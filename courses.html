Courses and Training

Are you considering using R in your team or organization, or taking your team's R skills to the next level? Then an on-site training course may be a valuable step in the right direction.

While there are many online resources for learning R, there are benefits to a more focused course/workshop where participants are provided with a common foundation for working effectively and collaboratively with R.

R is a very flexible and powerful language, and to get the most out of it, a solid understanding of the langauge syntax and idioms is essential.

Stefan offers training in R and RStudio, both at introductory and intermediate to advanced levels, and ad-hoc consulting tasks. For information on how a course can be structured to meet your organizations' needs you can contact Stefan either by Email or phone.
